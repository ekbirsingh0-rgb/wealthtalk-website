What is Super Visa Insurance and Why Do You Need It?

The Super Visa is a special entry visa designed specifically for parents and grandparents of Canadian citizens and permanent residents. It allows them to stay in Canada for up to two years without renewing their status. However, Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) mandates that all Super Visa applicants must have private health insurance coverage.

Super Visa insurance covers emergency medical expenses while your parents are visiting Canada. This includes hospital stays, emergency dental work, and emergency vision care. It provides essential protection against unexpected healthcare costs that could otherwise strain your family finances.

For many Calgary residents with parents in India, Punjab, or other parts of South Asia, Super Visa insurance is the gateway to having meaningful time with their aging parents. It's not just insurance—it's an investment in family time and peace of mind.

Super Visa Insurance Requirements in Calgary

The Government of Canada has specific requirements for Super Visa insurance that all applicants must meet:

  • Minimum coverage of $100,000 CAD for emergency medical care
  • Valid for the entire duration of stay in Canada (up to 24 months)
  • Coverage must be from a licensed Canadian insurance provider
  • Policy must cover emergency hospital visits and emergency medical expenses
  • Must be purchased before arrival in Canada
  • Proof of insurance required at port of entry

Choosing the Right Super Visa Insurance Provider

Not all Super Visa insurance policies are the same. Here's what to look for:

  • IRCC-approved providers: Ensure your insurer is recognized by Immigration Canada
  • Comprehensive coverage: Look beyond the minimum—some policies cover prescription drugs and vision care
  • Easy claims process: Your parents shouldn't struggle with complicated paperwork if an emergency occurs
  • Customer support: Choose a provider with 24/7 emergency support
  • No age limits: Some insurers limit coverage for parents over 75—verify this before purchasing

The Super Visa Application Process

Getting Super Visa insurance is straightforward:

  1. Contact an insurance broker or provider
  2. Provide your parents' ages, health information, and intended stay duration
  3. Receive a quote and policy documents
  4. Complete the application online or by mail
  5. Receive proof of insurance (required for IRCC application)
  6. Submit insurance proof with your Super Visa application

The entire process typically takes 3-5 business days. We recommend applying at least two weeks before your parents' travel date to avoid any last-minute complications.
