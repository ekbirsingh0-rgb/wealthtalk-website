What Is Super Visa Insurance and Why Is It Required?

Super Visa insurance is a specialized medical coverage plan designed specifically for parents and grandparents visiting Canada. This insurance must meet Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) requirements, which means it's not optional—it's a legal requirement for your parents' visa application.

The coverage must include:

  • Minimum coverage of CAD $100,000 for hospital and medical expenses
  • Coverage for emergency dental care (up to CAD $200)
  • Coverage for pre-existing medical conditions
  • A validity period matching their stay in Canada

Many families are surprised to learn that standard visitor insurance won't work—you need a plan that IRCC explicitly approves. This is where working with a licensed broker like Ekbir Singh makes all the difference.
