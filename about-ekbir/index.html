Quick Answer

Ekbir Singh is a licensed independent insurance broker based in Winnipeg, Manitoba, licensed in MB, AB, BC and ON. He helps Desi and newcomer families understand insurance and money in simple language — specializing in Super Visa, visitor and life insurance.

Meet Ekbir

I'm Ekbir Singh, a licensed insurance broker in Winnipeg. I work with Punjabi, Hindi and Desi families across Canada who want insurance explained clearly — without jargon, without pressure.

My approach: teach first, sell second. Calm, honest, older-brother energy. Bringing your parents to Canada or protecting your family should feel reassuring, not confusing. I explain insurance the way I'd explain it to my own family.

Credentials

  • Licensed insurance broker in Manitoba, Alberta, British Columbia and Ontario
  • Independent — I compare options from multiple Canadian and OSFI-authorized insurers
  • Fluent in English, Punjabi and Hindi
  • Based in Winnipeg, serving families across MB, AB, BC and ON by phone, video and digital
